About me

Ever since I was a kid, I've loved being creative. It all started with hours of drawing, then gradually making my own movies in Windows Movie Maker and later diving in to the world of photography. That spark pushed me to join the media program in high school. But after graduation, I felt lost, not sure where I was headed.

So, I took a leap into the unknown – traveling, trying out different jobs and even taking a psychology course in London. It was a game-changer, teaching me a ton about responsibility and independence.

As the years went by, I stumbled into the world of programming. My buddies who were into computer science nudged me to try it out. I took a Programming 1 class, and man, was I hooked! Programming felt like this cool mix of logic and creativity.

That passion led me to where I am now – a full-stack developer student at Chas Academy. Right now, I'm on the lookout for internships in development from December 2024 to June 2025. I'm pumped to share what I know and learn from other pros in the field.

Hope to hear from you and have a lovely day!
